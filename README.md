# Tomodoro — Support

Tomodoro is a simple pomodoro timer — plus a little pixel-pet farm that grows as you focus.

## Get help

- **Found a bug or have a question?** [Open an issue](../../issues/new)
  and I'll take a look.
- **Feature ideas** are welcome in issues too.

## FAQ

**I don't want the pets.**
Settings → Pets → turn off "Pet companion". The app becomes a plain
pomodoro timer. (Heads up: hidden pets still live their lives.)

**Does Tomodoro collect any data?**
No. There are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. Everything stays
on your device.
