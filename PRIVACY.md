# Tomodoro Privacy Policy

**Effective: July 17, 2026**

Tomodoro does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

- **No accounts.** The app has no sign-up, sign-in, or user profiles.
- **No data leaves your device.** Timers, settings, and pet progress
  are stored locally on your phone and nowhere else.
- **No analytics or tracking.** The app contains no analytics SDKs,
  advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.
- **No network access.** The app makes no network requests.

If a future update ever changes any of this (for example, optional
in-app purchases processed by Apple or Google), this policy will be
updated before that release, and payment processing would be handled
entirely by the platform stores — not by Tomodoro.

Questions? Open an issue on this repository.
